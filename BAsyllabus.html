BA-I Elective Subjects:-

In B.A-I year , every student have to select two subjects from following groups but every subject should be from different group.

Group-1	Group-2	Group-3	Group-4	Group-5
Hindi	Mathematics	Political science	Home Science	History
Sanskrit	Music	Physical Education		
Economics				


Note:- Music and home science cannot be taken together. Physical education is only for male department, not in female department .

Elective Subjects: Every student have to select two subjects from following:-

Hindi
Sanskrit
Economics
Mathematics
History
Political Science
Home Science

Note:-


Hindi is compulsory as Elective or Compulsory subject.
Student cannot take Music and Home Science together.

Environmental Studies is compulsory to qualify otherwise result and degree will not be provide to student.
Compulsory subject cannot taken as elective subject.
